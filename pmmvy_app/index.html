Maternal Health & IFA Dosage Reference Guide

Explore recommended guidelines for IFA supplements consumption, managing side effects, and checking hypothetical dosage guidelines using our calculator.

Essential IFA Intake Guidelines

  • Always consume the IFA tablet after meals (e.g. after dinner) to minimize mild side effects like nausea or stomach discomfort.
  • Drink a glass of lemon water or orange juice (Vitamin C) with the pill to dramatically enhance iron absorption.
  • Avoid taking the pill with tea, coffee, or milk, as calcium and tannins block iron absorption in the gut. Wait at least 1-2 hours before or after.
  • Do not worry if your stools appear dark/black. This is a completely harmless, normal result of unabsorbed iron.

Recommended Iron-Rich Foods

Complement your daily IFA supplements with foods rich in natural iron and folic acid:

🥦 Green Leafy Vegetables: Spinach, fenugreek (methi), amaranth.
🍇 Fruits & Nuts: Pomegranates, dates, figs, raisins, almonds.
🌾 Grains & Legumes: Chickpeas, lentils, millets (ragi), beans.
🍯 Natural Sweets: Jaggery (gur) and sesame seeds.
